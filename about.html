About Star Furnitures

Trusted Steel Furniture Manufacturers Since 2000

Our Story

Star Furnitures was established in the year 2000 by Sayyad Chand Basha. What began as a small family workshop has grown through honesty, dedication, and quality workmanship. Over the years, we have earned the trust of customers by manufacturing durable steel beruwas, steel racks, and supplying mirrors, iron sheets, and all related materials. Our goal has always been simple — provide quality products at fair prices while treating every customer with respect.

Our Mission

To manufacture quality steel furniture that lasts for years while providing excellent customer service.

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted steel furniture manufacturers in Andhra Pradesh.
